Our Matcha

Whisked with Isuzu 五十铃 matcha

We use Isuzu (五十铃) Japanese matcha — prized for its exceptionally low bitterness, deep umami, and a fragrant nori (seaweed-like) aroma. Smooth and mellow enough to enjoy on its own.

🌿

Powerful Antioxidant

Rich in catechins (EGCG) and tea polyphenols that help fight free radicals — supporting anti-ageing and healthy, glowing skin.

🧘

Calm, Steady Focus

Naturally contains L-theanine, which balances the caffeine for smooth, focused energy — a gentle lift without the jitters, palpitations, or crash.

⚡

Metabolism Support

Catechins may help support metabolism and fat burning. Pair it with low-fat milk or oat milk for a light, feel-good pick-me-up.

Best enjoyed as part of a balanced lifestyle · not intended as medical advice.
